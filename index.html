Cybersecurity: Information Security

Cybersecurity is the protection of information and its critical elements, including systems and hardware
that use, store, and transmit that information.
The primary mission of an information security program is to ensure that informations assets remain safe and useful.
Four important functions for an orgnaization:
Protecting the organization's ability to function, Protecting the data and information the organization collects and uses,
Enabling the safe operation of applications running on the organization's IT systems,
and Safeguarding the organizations technology assets .
